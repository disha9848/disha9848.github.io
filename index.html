About AquaCo

Established in 1992, our company is a leading manufacturer of High-Density Polyethylene (HDPE) and Polyvinyl Chloride (PVC) pipes and fittings. With a strong emphasis on quality, innovation, and efficient management practices, we have become a trusted name in the industry.

Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities and experienced team enable us to produce a wide range of pipes catering to various sectors.

Our company specialises in the manufacturing of HDPE and PVC pipes and fittings of various sizes and specifications, catering to a wide range of applications, including but not limited to:

  • Potable water supply systems as per IS: 4984:2016 & ISO: 4427
  • Unplasticized PVC Pipes for potable water supplies as per IS: 4985:2021
  • Unplasticized PVC (PVC-U) screen and casing pipes for bore/tubewell as per IS: 12818:2010
  • Sewerage and drainage systems as per IS: 14333:2022
  • Gas distribution networks as per IS: 14885:2022
  • Cable and telecom ducts as per TEC: 2019
